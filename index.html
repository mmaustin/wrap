Let Us Start With a Double Dose of Mrs. Barbara

In that inimitable, biting, yet fair, style of Barbara Tuchman, "The Guns of August" is a pulsating encapsulation of just how close Germany came to conquering Western Europe in the first weeks of what would become known as the Great War.

Anxious for America to enter the war and help break the deadly stalemate with Germany, the British found it when they intercepted "The Zimmerman Telegram."
